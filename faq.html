Common Questions

Frequently
Asked Questions.

Questions people ask before, during, and after working together — answered directly. If yours isn't here, the contact page goes straight to me.

Working Together
Are you available for freelance projects right now?+
Yes. I'm currently open to freelance, contract, and full-time opportunities with immediate availability. The fastest way to start a conversation is the contact page — I typically respond within 1 business day.
Do you work with international clients and across time zones?+
Yes, I work with clients globally and am comfortable coordinating across time zones using async communication (written updates, recorded walkthroughs) supplemented by overlapping-hours calls when needed.
Can you work within our existing brand guidelines and Storyline templates?+
Yes — this is the most common working arrangement. I'll build within your existing templates and brand guidelines. If I identify accessibility issues with the existing template, I'll flag them with recommended fixes, but the decision to address them remains yours.
What's your typical response time during an active project?+
Same business day for questions, within 24 hours for review feedback implementation status. I provide a weekly written status update on every active project regardless of whether there's a scheduled call.
Scope & Pricing
How do you price projects — hourly or fixed scope?+
Fixed-scope pricing for well-defined projects, based on a detailed brief and the Effort Estimator calculation. For projects with evolving requirements, I'll recommend an hourly or milestone-based arrangement instead — I'll always tell you honestly which pricing model fits your situation.
What's included in a typical eLearning development quote?+
Needs analysis, storyboarding, full development, accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1 AA), SCORM/xAPI packaging, up to 2 review cycles, and 30 days of post-launch support. Add-ons like localisation, custom animation, or AR/VR are quoted separately based on scope. See Services for the full breakdown.
Do you charge extra for accessibility compliance?+
No — WCAG 2.1 AA compliance is included as standard in every Full Development engagement, not an add-on. Retrofitting accessibility into an already-built course costs significantly more than building it in from the start, so it's priced into the base estimate.
What happens if the project scope changes mid-development?+
Scope changes happen on real projects. I document the original scope in the project brief, and any addition beyond it is quoted as a change order before work begins — so there's never a surprise invoice. Minor adjustments within the spirit of the original brief are absorbed without additional charge.
Technical & Process
Which authoring tools do you work in?+
Primarily Articulate Storyline 360 and Rise 360. Also experienced in Adobe Captivate, Lectora, iSpring, DominKnow, and Elucidat. For projects requiring capabilities beyond any authoring tool's native features, I build custom JavaScript interactions, AR/VR with ZapWorks, or 3D animation with Blender.
Do you handle SCORM and xAPI packaging and LMS testing?+
Yes. Every project includes packaging for the correct SCORM version, testing in SCORM Cloud, and verification in your actual target LMS before delivery — not just preview testing. See the LMS Demo and xAPI Demo for working examples.
Can you localise courses into multiple languages?+
Yes, including Arabic RTL. I manage the full localisation workflow — translation package preparation, audio recording coordination, RTL layout adaptation, and multi-language LMS packaging — across 16–22 languages depending on project scope.
Do you provide source files, or only the published output?+
Full source files (.story, raw media assets, documentation) are delivered at project close as standard — not held back. You own what you paid for.
Getting Started
I don't have a finished brief yet — can we still start a conversation?+
Yes — most projects start exactly this way. A discovery call helps clarify the actual training need before any brief is finalised. Reach out via the contact page with whatever you have, even if it's just "we have a problem and think training might help."
What do you need from me to provide an accurate quote?+
Ideally: the training topic, target audience, desired course duration, any existing content or materials, accessibility requirements, target LMS, and number of languages needed. Missing any of these is fine — we'll work through it together on a discovery call.
