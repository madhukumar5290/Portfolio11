What is Compliance Training?
Compliance training ensures employees understand and follow legal, regulatory, and company policies. Unlike skills training, compliance training is often mandatory and tracked for audit purposes.

The three pillars of compliance training are: Awareness (knowing the rules), Understanding (knowing why), and Application (doing the right thing in practice).
Slide 2 of 5 — Concept
Why Compliance Training Fails
Most compliance courses fail for the same reasons: they test recall, not application. Learners click through slides, pass a multiple-choice quiz about definitions, and forget everything within 48 hours.

Effective compliance training uses scenarios and branching decisions — placing learners inside realistic situations where they must apply the policy, not recite it.
